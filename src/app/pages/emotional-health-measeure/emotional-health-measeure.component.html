How to use your Emotional Health Graph?

Emotions are like a hidden hand that affect our thoughts and actions.

Being aware of our emotions is an important first step in strengthening our mind.

Some emotions are undesirable and unhealthy. They hide our true spirit that is always naturally happy. Be aware and remove them immediately when they appear, by simply focusing on the healthy emotions.
