This test is about your feelings, not your past behavior.   It's easy and fun to take, but only if you stop thinking and start feeling!

Remember the times you have acted on an intuition? When you were irritated or got angry at someone? Or
sent an email in a hurry, berating someone who disagreed with your views? Or sent a huge donation to save
hungry, dying pets? Did you “think” or you let your “feelings” dictate your actions?

Answer the questions as quickly as you can from your first impulse, your heart, not your brain! If you think, you will inadvertently rationalize your answers, and defeat the purpose of the test. It would become nothing more than yet another behavioral test, amongst hundreds out there!

After the first page, there are 10 more pages to go through. Try to complete them in no more than 10 minutes. As
you proceed, we will show you how much time is remaining, and how much of the test you have completed.
